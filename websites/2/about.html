Hi! My name is Carylann and I'm 6 years old! On July 6th 2006, my mom took me to a hospital to check if I had cancer! I was lucky, I don't have cancer... but i care for those other children who have cancer and i want to help them...

I will make hats for them so that those who lost their hairs can cover their head... I will make toys and games, and little teddy bears to keep them company... And i want to raise money for the hospital so that they can make research and find a cure for little children with cancer.

So I, my mom and her friends decided to open this website to help raise money and let people know what we are doing here. Please let us help them... I care!
